# Project Overview

This project analyzes Amazon's logistics and delivery performance across 2025 and 2026 to evaluate operational efficiency, identify delivery bottlenecks, and uncover opportunities to improve customer satisfaction.

Using **SQL** for data analysis and **Microsoft Excel** for dashboard development, the project transforms raw logistics data into interactive visualizations that highlight delivery performance across shipping methods, fulfillment hubs, product categories, and regions.

---

# Business Problem

Late deliveries can negatively impact customer satisfaction, increase operational costs, and reduce overall business performance.

This analysis was conducted to identify the key drivers of delivery delays and provide actionable recommendations to improve logistics efficiency and delivery reliability.

---

# Project Objectives

- Analyze delivery performance across 2025 and 2026.
- Measure on-time delivery performance and delayed deliveries.
- Evaluate shipping methods and fulfillment hub performance.
- Identify regions and product categories with the highest delivery delays.
- Compare delivery performance across both years.
- Provide data-driven recommendations for improving logistics operations.

# Key Insights

## 2025

- Processed **1,260 orders** with an **on-time delivery rate of 77.4%**.
- Same-Day Shipping recorded the highest number of delayed deliveries.
- Florida Hub contributed **25.5%** of all delayed deliveries.
- Beauty products experienced the highest delivery delays.
- Nevada recorded the highest regional delivery delays.

## 2026

- On-time delivery performance improved to **78.0%** while late deliveries decreased.
- Two-Day Shipping became the leading source of delivery delays.
- Florida Hub remained the largest contributor to delayed deliveries.
- Beauty products continued to experience the highest delivery delays.
- Texas recorded the highest regional delivery delays.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Improve operational efficiency at the Florida Fulfillment Hub.
- Optimize Same-Day and Two-Day shipping operations to reduce delivery delays.
- Strengthen inventory planning for high-demand product categories.
- Improve logistics planning in regions with persistent delivery delays.
- Continuously monitor delivery KPIs to support data-driven operational decisions.
